Point Values#

Each cipher has a base point value:

Cipher Base Points
Caesar Cipher 100 points
VigenÃ¨re Cipher 250 points

Bonus Points#

Additional points available for:

  • Speed Bonus - First 3 teams to solve each cipher (50/30/10 pts)
  • Clean Code Bonus - Well-documented, elegant solutions (up to 50 pts)

  • Document your attempts even if unsuccessful

â“ Frequently Asked Questions#

Registration & Eligibility#

Q: Do I need to register in advance?
A: No advance registration required. Just show up at the event desk on the day.

Q: I’m a freshman/non-CS major. Can I still participate?
A: Absolutely! All students are welcome, regardless of major or year.

Q: Can I participate solo?
A: Yes, solo participation is allowed, though teams are encouraged for collaborative learning.

Q: Is there an entry fee?
A: No, the event is completely free.

Technical Requirements#

Q: What should I bring?
A: Bring your laptop with your preferred programming environment set up. Chargers recommended!

Q: What programming languages can I use?
A: Any language you’re comfortable with. Python, Java, JavaScript, C++, etc. are all fine.

Q: Do I need special software?
A: No special software required. A text editor and programming language interpreter/compiler are enough.

Q: Will there be internet access?
A: Yes, WiFi will be available. You may use internet resources.

About the Challenges#

Q: How difficult are the codes?
A: They range from beginner-friendly (Caesar) to challenging (Mystery). All are solvable with the techniques taught on this website.

Q: Will we get hints?
A: Limited hints may be provided if teams are stuck, but try to solve independently first.

Q: Can we use online cipher-breaking tools?
A: You should implement your own solutions. Learning resources are fine; automated solvers are not.

Q: What if we can’t solve all the ciphers?
A: That’s okay! Solving even one or two is an achievement. Partial credit is awarded.

During the Event#

Q: Can we leave and come back?
A: Yes, but be mindful of the time limit. We recommend staying for the duration.

Q: Will food be provided?
A: Yes, refreshments and snacks will be available.

Q: Can we listen to music while working?
A: Yes, use headphones to not disturb others.

Q: Can we talk to other teams?
A: Socializing is fine, but don’t share solutions or specific approaches.

After the Event#

Q: Will solutions be published?
A: Yes, after the event, we’ll release detailed solution walkthroughs.

Q: Can we keep the code we write?
A: Yes, you own your code. We encourage you to share it (after the event) as learning resources.

Q: What if we disagree with our score?
A: You can request a score review within 15 minutes of results posting.
